Raised in Seattle, WA, I headed towards the sun for college. At UC San Diego, I studied cognitive science with a specialization in human computer interaction and minored in computer science before graduating in 2020!

I'm naturally curious, ready to explore and learn new things! I'm drawn to projects that allow me to use my creativity and innovation to solve logical problems, which is why I'm passionate about design. I get to integrate information, empathy and creativity to build meaningful experiences for people.

Currently, I'm working as a ui/ux design intern @ Five to Nine! Previously, I worked on projects with the UCSD Design Lab to create personalized experiences utilizing diabetes data & to reimagine the user interface of Dexcom's mobile app to improve CGM troubleshooting. I was also a Girls Who Code instructor where I got to teach high school girls computer science in a summer immersion program #girlpower.
