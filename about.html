Hi! I'm Allison 💫

Raised in Seattle, WA, I headed towards the sun for college. At UC San Diego, I studied cognitive science with a focus in design and interaction and minored in computer science before graduating in 2020!

I'm naturally curious, ready to explore and learn new things! I'm drawn to projects that allow me to use my creativity and innovation to solve logical problems, which is why I'm passionate about design. I get to integrate information, empathy and creativity to build meaningful experiences for people.

Currently, I'm working as a product designer @ Abound! Previously, I worked with 99 Drive improving the used car buying and selling experience, Five to Nine improving how organizer's create, manage, and share events & with the UCSD Design Lab creating tools to help people struggling with diabetes management. I was also a Girls Who Code instructor where I got to teach high school girls computer science in a summer immersion program #girlpower.
