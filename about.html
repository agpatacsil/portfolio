Hi! I'm Allison

I'm a product designer & iced latte lover based in NYC! Currently, I'm designing at Abound, an online two-sided marketplace, helping indie brands & retailers grow their business.

I'm naturally curious, so I'm always ready to explore and learn new things! I'm drawn to projects that allow me to use my creativity and innovation to solve logical problems. I truly love design because I get to integrate information, empathy and creativity to build meaningful experiences for people.

Previously, I worked with 99 Drive improving the used car buying and selling experience, Five to Nine improving how organizer's create, manage, and share events & with the UCSD Design Lab creating tools to help people manage their diabetes. I also worked as a Girls Who Code instructor teaching high school girls computer science in a summer immersion program #girlpower.
