Raised in gloomy Seattle, WA, I headed to sunny San Diego for college. I studied Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction at UC San Diego. And even minored in Computer Science before graduating in December 2019!

I'm naturally curious, ready to explore and learn new things! I'm drawn to projects that allow me to use my creativity and innovation to solve logical problems, which is why I'm passionate about design. I get to integrate information, empathy and creativity to build meaningful experiences for people.

Currently, I'm working with Dexcom and the Design Lab to redesign Dexcom's website, and previously worked with them to redesign the user interface of their mobile app. I was also a Girls Who Code instructor where I got to teach high school girls computer science in a summer immersion program #girlpower.
