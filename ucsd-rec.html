UCSD Recreation

Case Study | February 2018


Overview

The UCSD Recreation website is where people can look and sign up for events and classes hosted by UCSD Recreation. We observed the user flow of signing up for an event and aimed to minimize confusion and errors we observed in our redesign!

Responsibilities

  • Conducting user interviews
  • Analyzing interview data
  • Sketching wireframes
  • Creating high fidelity mock up

Problem

The UCSD recreation website (as of Winter 2018) is not user friendly making it difficult for people to navigate through the site pages to sign up for a recreation event.
