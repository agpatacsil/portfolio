About InsuLearn

Understanding how insulin affects blood glucose levels is important to diabetes management, but it can be an overwhelming concept when you're first learning! Our goal is to make this concept easier to digest and comprehend, so we created a tool that lets people use interactive graphs to see how different insulin doses and time of intake at mealtime affects blood glucose levels.
