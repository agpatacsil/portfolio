Daily UI Challenge

I decided to take on the popular 100 days of UI challenge, but there are a lot of articles mocking it and claiming that it won't help you become a better designer... so why am I doing it? I'm doing this to 1) improve my visual design and ui skills, 2) explore who I am as a designer by pushing myself to create something everyday, and 3) it gives me something to do while social distancing at home ☺️. I've included some of my favorite designs from the challenge here, but if you're interested in viewing all days, they're all available on my Dribbble.
