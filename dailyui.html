DAILY UI CHALLENGE

—Advancing my ui and visual design skills with 100 days of UI




I decided to take on the popular 100 days of UI challenge, but there are a lot of articles mocking it and and claiming that it won't help you become a better designer... so why am I doing it? 🤔

  • I want to improve my visual design and ui skills (typography, color, layout, hierarchy, etc.)

  • I live and breathe Figma, but I want to learn other popular design tools, such as Sketch, Invision, and Adobe XD, so I'm going to challenge myself to design with these other softwares!

  • As a new grad who hasn't found a job yet (contact me!), I'm bursting with ideas to explore and incredibly eager to create!

  • These daily designs aren't fully developed projects, but they will allow me to explore who I am as a designer by pushing me to create something every day.
